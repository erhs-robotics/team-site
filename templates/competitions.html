Competitions

The primary focus of our robotics club is to compete in robotics competitions. Our main competitions are:

  • FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC): The FRC is our largest competition. We compete in the FRC as Team 0053 - "Area 53"
  • VEX Robotics Competition: In additon to FRC, our team also competes in the Vex Robotics Competition, which is similar to FRC, but on a smaller scale
  • Mini-Urban Challenge: The Mini-Urban Challenge is a smaller competition (teams of 7 people) where the objective is to autonomously navigate a city
