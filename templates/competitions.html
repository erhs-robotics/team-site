Competitions

The primary focus of our robotics club is to compete in robotics competitions. Our main competitions are:

FIRST Robotics (FRC)

The FRC is our largest competition. We compete in the FRC as Team 0053 - "Area 53"

VEX Robotics

In additon to FRC, our team also competes in the Vex Robotics Competition, which is similar to FRC, but on a smaller scale

Mini-Urban Challenge

Mini-Urban Challenge: The Mini-Urban Challenge is a smaller competition (teams of 7 students) where the objective is to autonomously navigate a city
