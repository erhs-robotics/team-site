Eleanor Roosevelt High School

Robotics Club

The Eleanor Roosevelt Robotics Club is a group of about 50 bright students who come together to think of, design, and develop creative solutions to a wide variety of challenges in the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Field. We foster our students' interests in the STEM field through encouraging their use of problem-solving and critical thinking skills in a multitude of challenging ways, mainly through competing in various local robotics competitions

About

  1. Competition

    We are proud annual participants of the FIRST FRC competition as Team 53, "Area 53". We also compete in the ION Mini-Urban Challenge and the VEX Robotics Competition. This year, we will also our team also assists others in competitions such as FIRST's FIRST Lego League (FLL) competition.

  2. FIRST Robotics

    FIRST (For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is an organization dedicated to instilling the principles of the STEM field in today's youth. Their main competition, the First Robotics Competition (FRC) is held every year. A new competiton is anounced in early January every year, and every team is given six weeks to build a robot that can meet the challenges of competition. Click here for more information
