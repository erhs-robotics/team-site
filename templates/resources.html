Computer Science

  • Udacity

    A free online university offering top level instruction focusing on computer science. A vast array of courses are available, from basic introduction to Programming and Computer Science courses to advanced topics in Artificial Intelligence, Parallel Processing, Web Development, and Cryptography.

  • Learn Code the Hard Way

    A site with free textbooks to teach your how to code! Rather than bog you down with tons of theory, the exercises encourage you to explore the programming language by writing, debugging, and exploring programs. Programming languages include C, Python, Ruby, SQL, and Regex.
