ERHS Robotics

The ERHS Robotics Club is a group of about 50 bright students from Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Greenbelt, MD who come together to think of, design, and develop creative solutions to a wide variety of challenges in the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Field.

We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) as Team 53 - "Area 53"
